The minimalisitic exploration and exploitation task

  • The entire experiment will consist of 180 rounds(For demonstration purpose, here we only set 6 trials for each context).
  • In each task, you will act as a tourist exploring a foreign city, with the number of days varying from 5 to 10.
  • On the first day, you will randomly visit a restaurant to dine, and we will inform you of the restaurant's rating.
  • Afterwards, you can choose either “Go to a random new restaurant” or “Go back to the best restaurant so far”.
  • Ensure that after each selection you click the ✓ (confirm) button under both options.
  • Then we will inform you of the restaurant's rating.
  • We will accumulate the ratings of all the restaurants you chose during that round, and this sum will be your total score for that round.
